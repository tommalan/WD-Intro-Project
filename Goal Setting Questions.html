Goal Setting Questions


1. Why do I want to learn this skill?

I want to be develop this skill for my future career opportunities. It will also give me a chance to be creative.


2. What will I achieve if I learn this skill?

I will show that I can teach myself something a structured way. It would be the most successful academic and work-related venture for me since my dissertation.


3. How will this skill change my life and my career?

It will open up hundreds of different potential career paths I could follow. It will also give me the opportunity to work remotely through freelance work. I could get paid a good amount from only 15 hours a week of work and use this to travel the world.


4. How will learning this skill impact the lives of my family, friends and coworkers?

It will allow me to become financially independent so I no longer need to rely on my parents as a safety net. It will allow me to potentially help others with web related project etc. It could also serve as an insparation to friends who also want to learn to code like my mate Adam has been for me.


5. How will I feel if I never accomplish this?

I will feel that I have failed in moving forward in my life that I have been left behind. I wont get that feeling of setting out on this major goal and eventually getting there after months of hard work.


6. What would my life look like if I manage to accomplish this?

I can travel the world as much as I want for as long as I want. I could use extra money to move to new awesome cities in the medium or long term. I will be able to move and live where I want, I will not have to move back home. I will have built many great projects and shown of my creative skills. With my new found confidence in independent learning I will find it easier to pick up additional skills.

Goals For Web Development

Looking at what I want to achieve in this pursuit

After my encounters with many successful programmers at home and on my travels I have decided to learn web development. This is a useful for jobs I may wish to undertake in the future. It's also translatable as it will give me a basic understanding of how the web works and a basic idea of how to code. This page will show the benefits of learning web development skills in greater detail.

Identified Goals

  • Have increased earning potential in the medium and long term.

    • To secure savings for my long term future.
    • Have the income to give me freedom of choices in what I want to do. Ie see this event, go travelling here etc.
    • To improve my quality of life ie good food, laptop, gym etc.
    • Have enough financial resources to avoid living at home under any circumstances.

  • Have the freedom to work anywhere in the world, either through job opportunities or freelance remote work.

    • ie move to somewhere like New York or Sydney for a programming job when I'm older.
    • Earn decent money per hour worked anywhere online.

  • Give me a platform to be creative and express my hobbies.

    • For example to build my own website or business.

  • Open up new learning opportunities and potential career moves in the future.

    • To learn different types of code and understand emerging technologies better.
    • Could allow to transtion into management further down the road at tech companies.
